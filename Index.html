Allerg.io

Preventing Allergic Reactions Since 2017
Allerg.io is a fun, intuitive app with a user-friendly interface for people with allergies Did you know that 15 million Americans suffer from some sort of food allergy. Our app solves this troublesome problem with only a few simple steps. Take a picture of the food you are unsure about to see if it contains anything which may be harmful to you, then, our app will let you know what ingredients make up the food. You can also manually select what cusine you would prefer and select your allergies. The result will be a delicious meal which will not contain any foods to provoke your allergie
